The View at Kew

Welcome to The View at Kew, a gallery of photographs I took at the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew. Located in Richmond, UK, only thirty minutes outside Central London, the three-hundred-acre UNESCO World Heritage Site has one of the most diverse collections of living plants of any botanic garden in the world.

Plan your visit at Kew.org.
